

 Mtaziz:

Ingredients:

1 onion     
garlic      
some spices        
brown flour      
chicken or beef      
vegetables          
water          
salt

instructions :

1)put the water with brown flour in a mixing bowl and mix them together until become dough.

2)Make dough like pancake.

3)Put the pot in the oven with the onion, salt, and spices for 5 minutes.

4)Add the meat or chicken and water leave it in the oven until the meat cooks for ½ hour.

5)Add the pancake with vegetables until it boil and then put on low heat for 20 minutes “without moving”.

6)Take the matziz out of the pot and enjoy!

Calories:507
