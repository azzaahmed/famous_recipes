SERVINGS 3-4
Calories 1760
Time needed :35mins

INGREDIENTS
•	3lbs beef short ribs
•	salt and pepper

DIRECTIONS
1.	Season the meat with salt and pepper.
2.	Grill on a gas or charcoal grill over medium heat 30 minutes.
3.	You can also roast the meat in an oven at 300°F for 1¼ hours.
4.	Meat should be golden brown, tender and juicy when ready.
5.	Nyama choma goes well with kachumbari or cooked greens.
